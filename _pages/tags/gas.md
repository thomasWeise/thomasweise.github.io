Genetic Algorithms&nbsp;(GAs) are Evolutionary Algorithms&nbsp;({% include taglink.liquid tag="EAs" %}) applied to bit-string based search spaces.
In other words, a GA is basically an EA specialized to search spaces&nbsp;${\mathbb{X}}=\{0,1\}^n$.
