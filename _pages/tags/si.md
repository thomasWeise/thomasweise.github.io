Swarm Intelligence&nbsp;(SI) is the research field of {% include taglink.liquid tag="metaheuristic" %} that use simplified variants of swarm behavior to solve optimization problems.
It is a field of Evolutionary Computation&nbsp;({% include taglink.liquid tag="ec" %}).
The most well-known swarm intelligence methods are Ant Colony Optimization&nbsp;({% include taglink.liquid tag="aco" %}) and Particle Swarm Optimization&nbsp;({% include taglink.liquid tag="pso" %}).
